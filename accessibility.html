Accessibility

We want every guest to enjoy Snelgrove — online and in our shops.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Snelgrove Ice Cream is committed to making our website and our shops accessible to as many people as possible, including guests with disabilities. We believe everyone deserves a sweet, welcoming experience.

Our Commitment

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive needs.

What We're Doing

  • Using clear, readable typography and color combinations chosen for sufficient contrast.
  • Providing descriptive alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Structuring pages with proper headings and landmarks so they work well with screen readers.
  • Supporting keyboard navigation and visible focus states for interactive elements.
  • Designing responsive layouts that adapt to phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Including a "skip to content" link and descriptive labels on forms and buttons.

In Our Shops

Our team is happy to assist guests who need help reading the menu, navigating our locations, or placing an order. If there is anything we can do to make your visit easier, please ask a team member.

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time project. We continue to review and improve our website and welcome feedback that helps us do better. Some third-party content or tools embedded on our site may not yet be fully accessible, and we work with our partners to address those gaps.

Need Help or Have Feedback?

If you encounter any difficulty using our website, or if you have suggestions for how we can improve, please let us know. We will do our best to provide the information or assistance you need.
