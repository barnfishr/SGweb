Our Heritage

A Nickel Cone
That Changed
Everything.

In 1929, Charles Rich Snelgrove set out to create something special — "artistry in pleasure foods." During one of America's toughest times, he crafted ice cream as an affordable luxury: a nickel cone that could bring families together, share laughter, and offer a taste of hope.

Nearly a century later, his granddaughter Lyndsay is carrying the torch — bringing those same classic flavors back to Utah, one scoop at a time.
