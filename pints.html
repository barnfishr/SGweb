Find Our Pints

Snelgrove Ice Cream pints are now available at grocery stores across Utah's Wasatch Front. Bring home your favorite flavors.

Your Favorite Flavors,
Now in Pint Form.

We've taken the same hand-crafted recipes that have been delighting Utah families since 1929 and packed them into pints you can take home. Made with real milk and cream, every pint is a taste of Snelgrove nostalgia — straight from your freezer.
