Why Snelgrove?

Own a Piece of
Utah History.

Snelgrove Ice Cream isn't just another ice cream brand — it's a Utah institution. Founded in 1929 by Charles Rich Snelgrove, the brand was built on "artistry in pleasure foods" and became the state's most beloved ice cream for generations.

Now revived by his granddaughter Lyndsay, Snelgrove is growing again — and franchise opportunities mean you can bring this iconic brand to your community. With proven recipes, fierce brand loyalty, and a story that sells itself, a Snelgrove franchise is more than a business — it's a legacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

The total initial investment for a Snelgrove Ice Cream franchise varies based on location, size, and build-out requirements. Contact our franchise team for a detailed breakdown and to review our Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD).
While food service experience is a plus, it's not required. We're looking for passionate operators who love community, have strong business acumen, and are committed to delivering a great customer experience. Our comprehensive training program covers everything you need to know.
Franchisees receive full support from day one — including site selection assistance, store design and build-out guidance, hands-on operations training, marketing resources, supply chain setup, and ongoing business coaching. We're invested in your success.
We're currently focused on expanding throughout Utah and across the Intermountain West. If you have a specific market in mind, let us know — we're eager to explore new communities where Snelgrove can thrive.
Heritage. Snelgrove isn't a startup — it's a nearly 100-year-old brand with deep roots, generational loyalty, and recipes perfected over decades. Combine that with a modern operating model, strong community identity, and multiple revenue streams, and you have a franchise that's both timeless and forward-looking.
The typical timeline from signing your franchise agreement to opening day is approximately 6 to 12 months, depending on site availability, permitting, and build-out in your area. Our team guides you through every step to keep things on track.
